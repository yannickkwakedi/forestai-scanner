## 🚀 What It Does

ForestAI Scanner helps Canadian small and medium businesses assess their AI readiness in minutes. Instead of paying expensive consultants for a preliminary evaluation, SME owners answer a series of targeted questions and receive a personalized AI adoption report instantly.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🤖 **Claude API integration** — AI-powered analysis and recommendations
- 🌐 **Bilingual** — Full French and English toggle
- 📄 **PDF report download** — Instant downloadable assessment report
- 📧 **Automated email reports** — Delivered via Resend
- 📊 **Admin dashboard** — Track submissions and insights
- 📱 **Mobile responsive** — Works on all devices

---

## 🛠️ Built With

- **Claude API** (Anthropic) — AI analysis engine
- **Replit Agent 4** — Development and deployment
- **Resend** — Email delivery
- **jsPDF** — PDF generation
- **Vanilla JS / HTML / CSS** — Frontend

---

## 🎯 The Problem It Solves

Most Canadian SMEs know AI matters but don't know where to start. ForestAI Scanner removes the barrier — no technical knowledge required, no expensive consultant needed. Just honest, actionable insight in minutes.

---

## 🍁 Built for Canada

ForestAI is built specifically for the Canadian SME market — bilingual, PIPEDA-aware, and designed for business owners who are ready to compete in the AI economy.
